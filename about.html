About Us

WHO WE ARE

At Vidharth, we are one of the best event and exhibition planning companies all across the globe.

In a time where more and more companies are hosting events and exhibitions, it can be challenging to get a message across to the audience. Therefore, we at Vidharth, take a thoughtful and creative style to develop unforgettable experiences that stir up feelings, sentiments, and conversations.

We, at Vidharth, not only specialize in producing ground-breaking, classy, and breathtaking events and exhibitions but also use our approach to create custom Stand Design, AV Films, and Retails Interiors.

We, boast of a diverse team of event and exhibition planners, media, and PR people. So, no matter what kind of a need your business has, we pride ourselves on delivering a first-class service to each and every one of our clients.

Our Mission
  • Our mission is to create the world's most meaningful and memorable experiences, and to meet and exceed the expectations of our clients through ground-breaking concepts and the delivery of outstanding service.
Our Vision
  • To be the top Event Exhibitions Management Company, and help our clients attain their goal through the development and execution of tailored and accurately planned events and exhibitions.
Our Principals
  • At Vidharth, we always put your organization first. We approach every project with care and accuracy. Irrespective of the size and scope, we treat every event and exhibition as an opportunity to display your brand, thus, develop an all-inclusive plan to make sure that your event and exhibition happen on time and within budget. At Vidharth, we make it a point to first learn about your business, we understand your challenges, and we plan events and exhibitions to fulfill your objectives.
Our Team
  • Our team of highly experienced and dedicated professionals at Vidharth understands that a well-executed event and exhibition can help an organization or an individual to achieve their strategic vision, and gain more and more clients. That is why our experts try their best to get to know your company inside and out and manage each aspect to organise excellent event and exhibition.
Our strength
  • We have a highly experienced team of veteran industry professionals, which makes sure that your event and exhibition goes right the first time
  • We are proficient when it comes to designing the entire event and exhibition and including important branding elements flawlessly.
  • We are completely transparent and will never make false promises to our clients.
  • We have a huge pool and vendors and other important people in our contact
  • An event and/or exhibition planned by us will give your sales team an opportunity to shine and attract the right people.
  • Our huge experience in the industry ensures that all your employees and clients will always be happy with you.
